Goals

  • Translate appropriate chapters of Rambam into English. The translation does not have to be literal; literal translations are already available (Yale, Touger). The goal of our translation is to make the text complete and accessible for collaboration by people not proficient enough in Hebrew.

  • Reconstruct the models and formulae implied in the text.

  • Implement the algorithms.

  • Verify consistency of Rambam's numbers.

  • Compare Rambam's models with contemporary astronomical models.
