Original code relies on the ServiceLoader mechanism, and places provider-configuration
files in resources/META-INF/services. Here, ServiceLoader mechanism is not used, and
JEuclidFopFactoryConfigurator class provides static method configure() that achieves
equivalent results programmatically for a specific FopFactory. No provider-configuration
files are included, but here is the list of their names and contents for informational purposes:

  org.papache.fop.fo.ElementMapping                             in it: net.sourceforge.jeuclid.fop.JEuclidElementMapping
  org.apache.fop.render.XMLHandler                              in it: net.sourceforge.jeuclid.fop.JEuclidXMLHandler

  org.apache.xmlgraphics.image.loader.spi.ImageConverter        in it: net.sourceforge.jeuclid.xmlgraphics.ImageConverterMathML2G2D
  org.apache.xmlgraphics.image.loader.spi.ImageLoaderFactory    in it: net.sourceforge.jeuclid.xmlgraphics.ImageLoaderFactoryMathML
  org.apache.xmlgraphics.image.loader.spi.ImagePreloader        in it: net.sourceforge.jeuclid.xmlgraphics.PreloaderMathML
